Concept J — Nib Variants

Four nib proportions × four real fountain pen ink palettes. The nib shape gets progressively narrower top-to-bottom. All use the same underline mechanic — nib tip drops into an ink line that runs beneath the full wordmark.

J1 Ink Navy Narrow shoulder (20px). Classic royal blue — the colour of a Waterman or Pilot cartridge. Clean, confident.
J2 Bottle Green Slimmest nib (14px). A vintage British-ink green — Diamine Woodland Green territory. Elegant and a little unexpected.
J3 Sepia Amber 18px shoulder with curved sides — softer than the angular options. Warm amber ink feels literary and handcrafted, like aged manuscript.
J4 Violet Ink Narrowest-but-tallest nib (16px × 64px) — the most pen-like proportion. Violet ink is a real fountain pen classic and reads creative without being aggressive.
