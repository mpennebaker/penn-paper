Five directions for the penn & paper games mark. Each is shown on light and dark backgrounds. Tell me which one feels right, or if you want to mix elements from a few.

A Lettermark The current direction. Compact, punchy — lets the ampersand do all the work.
B Editorial Stacked Wordmark Typographic, magazine-influenced. "PENN" bold + red rule + "& paper" creates a strong editorial feel.
C Dark Badge Contained, badge-like. Works great as an icon, app splash, or merch stamp. High contrast.
D Paper Fold Mark A geometric dog-eared page — direct nod to "paper". Icon-friendly, scales to any size. Versatile.
E Pixel P Monogram Pixel-art "P" as the mark — ties directly to the pixel puzzle game. Playful but structured.
