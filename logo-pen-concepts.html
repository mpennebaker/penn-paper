Pen on Paper — Logo Concepts

Five directions that put a literal pen-on-paper moment into the mark. Each shown on light and dark. The wordmark uses Space Grotesk to match the rest of the site.

F Fountain Pen Nib Classic nib silhouette with lined paper below — instantly reads "pen on paper." Sophisticated, scales to any size.
G Pencil on Ruled Paper A geometric pencil crossing three ruled lines — casual, approachable, unmistakably "writing." The red eraser and ink dot add the accent colour.
H Nib Writing the Ampersand The nib is mid-stroke, with the studio's own & as the ink it's producing. Connects the pen imagery directly to the brand name.
I Pen on Dog-Eared Page Combines the paper fold mark with a pencil crossing it diagonally — both elements in one icon. Literal and complete.
J Nib + Ink Line Underline The nib tip drops into a red ink line that becomes the underline of the full wordmark. Nib and name are one continuous visual stroke.
